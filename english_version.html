Shakuhachi Player Kodo Ishibashi

Born in Tokyo in 2000. Participated in a hands-on class hosted by the Setagaya San-kyoku Association and began learning shakuhachi at the age of 9 under the guidance of Haruto Fujii.

Graduated from the Tokyo University of the Arts Music High School and Department of Japanese Traditional Music, currently enrolled in the master's program of the same university.

During my studies, I have trained under Seibo Aoki, Ko Mei Takemura, Yoji Kawase, and other renowned teachers in the Koto-Koryu and Tozan-Ryu schools.

While at university, she received the "Ataka Prize", "Ryohei Miyata Prize", "Doho-kai Prize", and "Acanthus Music Prize".

she performed at the Imperial Palace’s Tokyo Music Hall in the "2022 Graduation Concert of Music Universities."

she is actively involved in both traditional and contemporary music, including pop music, expanding my performance repertoire.

Member of the Nihon Sankyoku Association, Koto-Koryu Association, Setagaya Sankyoku Association, and Doho-kai.
